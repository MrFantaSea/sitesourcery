Who is responsible

Site Sourcery is an alternate name used by Desiderata Labs LLC. Desiderata Labs LLC is responsible for the information covered by this notice. Privacy requests can be sent to hello@sitesourcery.com or made by calling (321) 788-2555.

What this website collects

  • Held form fields: the inquiry pages display fields for project planning, but their submission controls are disabled. We do not receive or save what you type into those held forms.
  • Catalog context: website inquiries include the non-binding planning identifiers SS-TIERS-2026.3 and SS-ADDONS-2026.3 so we can identify which footprint, capability, transition, eligibility, and cap rules you saw. Selecting an item does not create a quote, order, reservation, or authority to begin work.
  • Messages you send directly: the contents and routing details of calls, texts, and emails you choose to send us.
  • Basic site-delivery and security data: the hosting provider may process IP address, browser, device, timestamp, request, and anti-abuse information needed to deliver and protect the static site.
  • Your display preference: this site stores a theme setting in your browser’s local storage. It is not sent to us and is not used to track you.

Do not type or send passwords, login or recovery codes, API keys, payment or bank details, customer lists, health information, DNS secrets, or other private provider records through the held forms, ordinary email, or text.

This website does not intentionally run advertising pixels, behavioral analytics, or cross-site tracking cookies.

Why we use it

We use information you choose to send directly to answer your inquiry, discuss possible scope, prevent abuse, keep appropriate business records, and meet legal obligations. We do not use an inquiry as permission for unrelated marketing, and an inquiry does not authorize commercial work.

Who processes it

  • GitHub Pages hosts and delivers this static website.
  • Our email and communications providers transmit and store the messages needed to reply to you.
  • Professional advisers or authorities receive information only when reasonably necessary for accounting, legal compliance, security, or the protection of rights.

We do not sell or rent personal information. We do not disclose it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

How long we keep it

We aim to delete an inquiry that does not become a project no later than 24 months after the last interaction. If you become a customer, we retain the project, transaction, consent, tax, and dispute records needed for the relationship and applicable legal periods. Providers may keep short-lived security logs and backups under their own retention schedules.

Your choices

You may ask us to confirm whether we hold your information, provide a copy, correct it, or delete it. You may also withdraw consent for future contact. We aim to complete a verified deletion request within 30 days unless a legal obligation or active dispute requires a limited record to be retained. We may ask for enough information to verify that the request is really yours.

Automation projects and your customers

This notice covers Site Sourcery’s own website and inquiries. The Hive is not currently a public production service. Before any future automation deployment, the project would need to identify who controls customer information, the enabled providers and data flows, permissions, access controls, retention, deletion, opt-out handling, incident response, and project-specific processing terms.

Children, security & changes

This business website and its inquiry forms are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly seek their personal information. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage system is perfectly secure. We will update the date and this notice before materially changing how the forms or production services use information.
