No vanity metrics, no inflated “impressions.” The Meter counts things a business owner actually cares about, each measured the plain way.

There’s a color on the Meter you’ll never see on this page: a green we reserve for the truth. A sample can’t wear it. Only your real, verified month can.

A note, not a numbers app

You never log into anything.

The whole idea is that you keep running your business, not a marketing console. The Meter comes to you — first week of the month, in your inbox, in plain words. Read it in a minute. Reply to a human. That’s it.

Proof you can read in a minute, sent every month — whether the news is good or bad. That’s the difference between a vendor and a partner.

Anyone can show you a chart that only goes up. A partner shows you the real month — and when something slips, tells you why before you have to ask. The Meter is how we stay honest out loud, every thirty days, for as long as you’re with us. It’s also why leaving is easy: there’s never a mystery about what you’re paying for.
