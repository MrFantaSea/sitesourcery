Welcome

Buon appetito.

Family-owned since 2009, Trattoria Bella Vita brings a little corner of Italy to the heart of Collingswood. Our pasta is rolled by hand every morning, our mozzarella is pulled fresh, and our wood-fired oven has been going strong since day one. Come hungry — leave like family.
