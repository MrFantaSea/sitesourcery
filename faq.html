§08

The Marginalia

Straight answers, in the margins.

The honest back-matter — the real questions people ask before they trust a stranger with their name online, answered plainly. Nothing dodged, no fine print doing the dodging for us. Prefer a person? That door's right here.

¶ 01

Is it really custom, or a template with my logo on it?

Genuinely custom. Every Build+Care site is drawn from scratch for your trade, your town, and your real photos — and we check each one against every other site we've made, so no two come out alike.

The open-studio self-serve tier builds you a real new version each time, not a bespoke commission a person art-directs — and we're careful to call that exactly what it is.

¶ 02

Who actually builds it — a robot?

The sorcery is the AI; the promise is the person. Frontier AI does the heavy lifting — the same craft behind daarx.money — and then a real person gives it a final once-over for taste and truth before a word of it goes live under your name.

Nothing half-baked, and nothing invented, ever ships unattended. That once-over is the whole reason to trust the rest.

¶ 03

Do I own it?

Yes — and precisely: the self-contained site file and your domain are yours, genuinely portable, and we help you take them once the build's paid off. It's your name; you should hold the keys.

¶ 04

What happens if I cancel?

You cancel anytime, and the static file and domain stay yours. What stops: hosting, care, text-to-change, and anything that runs on our accounts — booking, call-tracking, live review pulls — because those live on our keys, not yours.

No exit fee, and never a file held for ransom. We'd rather you stay because it works than because you're stuck.

¶ 05

How is it this cheap? What's the catch?

For twenty years a genuinely-custom site meant a $3,000–5,000 agency and weeks of waiting. AI collapsed the labor — not the quality.

There's no catch: we don't sell ads, we don't resell your data, there's no lock-in. We earn on the monthly care, so we only win if your site keeps working for you.

¶ 06

What's the catch with the 30-day trial?

None. We take a card to begin — then you've got 30 days. If it isn't right, cancel inside the month and you're not out a cent. Risk-free means risk-free.

¶ 07

Will AI actually recommend me?

Here's the honest version: nobody can promise a ranking, and anyone who does is selling you something. What we can do is build the most machine-legible, quotable site in your market — the one an assistant can actually read and cite — and prove every month what it's doing: calls, bookings, where you rank, who's citing you.

We control the site and the proof. We stay honest about the rest.

¶ 08

Do I have to log into a dashboard?

Never. You don't touch a line of code or a control panel. Text us a change and we make it; once a month, one plain note lands — the Meter. That's the whole relationship.

¶ 09

I already have a website or domain. Can you use it?

Yes. We rebuild it properly and bring your domain across — we handle the transfer. You keep your address; it just finally points at a site that earns it.
