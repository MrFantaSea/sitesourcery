There’s no dashboard, no app, and nothing to learn. That’s the whole point.

Money & ownership

What’s yours, in writing

Do I own my site?

Yes. The design, the words, the photos, and your domain are yours. If you ever leave, we hand everything over and help you move it — no exit fee, no files held hostage.

We’d rather you stay because it’s working than because you’re stuck.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, with one tap — no contracts, no cancellation window, no phone-tree maze. What stops is what we run for you: hosting, changes by text, and the monthly note. The site itself and your domain stay yours.

How does the 30-day trial work?

Every done-for-you plan starts the same way: card upfront, one build, 30 days risk-free. If it isn’t right, cancel with one tap inside the month and pay nothing. If you keep it, the trial converts to your plan automatically.

No surprise terms — that’s the whole deal.

What does it cost?

There’s a rung for every budget: drive it yourself from $9/mo, done-for-you at $89/mo with $0 down, own-it builds from $500 plus a low monthly, up to White Glove Wizardry by application.

Every done-for-you plan includes the 30-day risk-free trial. The pricing page lays out the whole ladder.

Being found

The honest part

Can you guarantee I show up first on Google?

No — and anyone who promises that is lying to you. Nobody controls Google, and no honest shop pretends to.

What we actually do: build your site with the setup that makes it findable and citable — fast pages, clean structure, the details search and answer tools look for — so you have your best shot at being found and recommended when someone asks for the best plumber or bakery near them.

Then the monthly note shows how it’s actually moving: calls, bookings, where you rank. Results vary; honesty doesn’t.

How do I know it’s working?

Once a month you get a plain-English note — “14 calls, 6 booked, here’s where you rank” — no charts to decode, no login to check. If a number dips, the note says so, and what we’re doing about it.

Real numbers only. Results vary, and we never guarantee them.
