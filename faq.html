Day to day

How it works

How do I change my site?

Send an eligible Care request in plain English by text or email. Host includes no content edits; the other Care plans have the exact monthly limits listed below.

There’s no dashboard or app to learn. The paid website build remains yours whether or not you keep Care.

Who am I actually working with?

Site Sourcery, directly. A real person on a real number who knows your site, answers when available, and returns messages — the same point of contact every time.

Everything shown on this site is work I actually built.

I already have a website or domain. Can you use it?

Yes. I can rebuild the site and point your existing domain to it. Your domain stays in an account you own and control; the signed Scope & Job Manifest records the exact move and any provider dependencies.

Is every website tier built to the same quality?

Yes. Every tier gets the same baseline of custom craft, phone-first care, accessibility, performance, and clear technical structure. A smaller tier is not a lower standard of work.

Higher tiers scale ambition and included caps: more pages, content depth, custom art, motion, tools, integrations, and revision rounds. Your signed Scope & Job Manifest lists the exact deliverables before payment.

How do revisions and change orders work?

One revision round is one grouped feedback list from the named decision maker. A revision adjusts work within the signed scope. A bug is a reproducible failure against that scope, and fixing it does not use a revision round.

Added pages, custom art or motion, a new tool or integration, a new direction, or a major restructure are new scope. We start those only after you approve a written paid change order or tier upgrade.

Can I buy a website add-on by itself?

No. Every add-on requires an eligible base website tier and is a capped one-time adjustment — not a standalone product, Care allowance, or Hive workflow. An order can use at most one scope-expansion add-on type.

You may flag items in an inquiry, but only the signed Scope & Job Manifest accepts an add-on, quantity, prerequisite, and price. A new capability class, second tool, major art or motion system, CMS or backend, Hive workflow, or Flagship overflow requires the proper tier or Beyond scope. Provider and license fees remain separate. See the public add-on ledger.

The Responder · pre-launch concept

A planned workflow for missed calls

What is The Responder?

It is a proposed automation workflow: after an unanswered call, an approved text would be sent where provider, carrier, consent, and legal requirements allow it. It is not operating as a public production service.

The displayed $99/mo figure is a planning estimate, not a current offer. Existing-number support, delivery timing, and availability depend on provider and carrier approval. See the requirements and pre-launch status.

Is it a bot pretending to be a human?

That is a launch requirement: an automated message must not impersonate a person. Any future configuration must use approved content, honor opt-outs, provide a human follow-up path, and be monitored for failures.

No production claim is made until those controls are implemented and tested.

Can I try it now?

Not yet. There is no public Responder demo or production onboarding. The published phone number is a normal contact line, not an automated trial.

A trial will be advertised only after carrier registration, provider setup, consent and opt-out tests, delivery/failure tests, monitoring, and security review pass.

Money & ownership

What’s yours, in writing

Do I own my site?

Yes — once the build is paid in full. Site Sourcery’s original custom deliverables for the approved scope are yours. Third-party and client-supplied assets keep their existing licenses. Your domain and provider accounts stay in your name and under your control.

I’d rather you stay because it’s working than because you’re stuck.

Can I cancel?

Website Care is month-to-month and cancellable by text or email. At the end of the paid month, hosting, backups, updates, changes, and other plan features stop. You keep the paid website files and domain control, and we will help move the site to hosting you control.

The Hive is not currently operating, so there is no Responder subscription to cancel. Any future Hive agreement must state cancellation, data export, retention, provider, and shutdown terms before activation.

What does it cost?

Custom websites are buy-once: eight tiers from $200 (Card) up to $3,000 (Flagship), and you own the completed build on final payment — no subscription to rent your own site. Optional Care runs from $15/mo to $159/mo. Host is managed website care with no content edits; change capacity begins with Care Lite.

The Responder and other Hive workflows are not currently for sale. Their $99/mo starting point and $219/mo bundle are planning estimates for feedback. An inquiry is non-binding; the pricing page keeps those estimates separate from current website pricing.

Who owns the accounts, and who pays provider or license fees?

You own and control your domain, hosting, payment, email, analytics, and other provider accounts. You pay registrar, provider, transaction, usage, and license fees unless the signed Scope & Job Manifest expressly includes them.

Third-party assets keep their original licenses. Site Sourcery’s original custom deliverables transfer to you after final payment.

What exactly is included in each Care plan?
  • Host · $15/mo: hosting, security, and backups; no content edits.
  • Care Lite · $29/mo: up to two micro edits or 30 minutes total per month, whichever limit is reached first.
  • Care · $59/mo: up to four eligible edits or 60 minutes total per month, whichever limit is reached first, plus a plain-English monthly performance note.
  • Care Plus · $99/mo: up to two total service hours plus one agreed findability or review-support action per month.
  • Partner · $159/mo: up to three total service hours, a proactive monthly review, one quarterly refresh, and one 30-minute call per month.

Optional overflow: at most one pack may be approved per website per paid month. Care Lite is $50 for up to two more micro-edits or 30 minutes; Care is $95 for up to four more ordinary existing-content edits or 60 minutes; Care Plus or Partner is $95 for up to 60 more minutes of otherwise-eligible work. Host has no overflow pack.

An overflow pack never adds another findability action, call, proactive review, quarterly refresh, page, feature, or broader capability.

A micro edit is one small supplied change, such as business hours, a price, a short copy correction, or one supplied image. New pages, redesigns, custom art, motion, tools, and integrations are new scope and need a written change order.

Nothing unused rolls over — not edits, minutes, hours, actions, refreshes, or calls.

Being found

The honest part

Can you guarantee I show up first on Google?

No — and anyone who promises that is lying to you. Nobody controls Google, and no honest shop pretends to.

What I actually do is build a strong local foundation: fast pages, clean structure, and your name, town, hours, services, and contact paths written clearly for people and search systems. I never promise a #1 ranking; placement and results vary.

Where a Care plan includes findability or review support, we use available, supportable data and state what it can and cannot prove. Results vary; honesty doesn’t.

How do I know it’s working?

Care ($59/mo) and the higher Care plans include a monthly plain-English note using available, supportable data — no charts to decode and no login to check.

A tap does not prove a completed call, and example numbers shown on this site are illustrations. Results vary and are never guaranteed. See how the note works.
