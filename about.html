The division of labor

The machine guarantees the floor. The human guarantees the ceiling.

The floor is what every site must have and no one should have to think about: fast, accessible, mobile, machine-legible, self-contained, honest markup — every single time. The AI holds that floor without tiring.

The ceiling is the part that's worth a person: that it looks right, sounds like you, and says nothing you can't stand behind. A machine can't care whether a claim is true. I can, so I do — on every site, before it's yours.

That division is also why this costs what it costs. AI collapsed the labor that made a genuinely-custom site a $5,000 project. I kept the custom, and passed the rest to you.

On the tool itself: what matters is the craft and the person behind it, not a brand name to drop. So I don't name the model — I put my name on the result instead.
