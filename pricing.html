Chapter I · Conjure once

The planning model starts with a verified footprint. If a project is later accepted and finally paid, Site Sourcery’s original custom deliverables for that approved scope transfer to the client; supplied and third-party material keeps its existing ownership or license.

HOW A REBUILD IS PRICEDSCOPE BEFORE PIXELS

1 · CURRENT FOOTPRINTWe inventory the public pages, locations, menus, collections, forms, and providers already serving the business. Until an intentional restructure is approved, that footprint is the pricing floor.

2 · DELIVERED FOOTPRINTThe base tier pays for the public pages in the approved new site. Every old URL is kept, merged into a named destination, or retired with a reason and redirect plan.

3 · CAPABILITY + TRANSITIONBespoke visuals, motion, tools, provider setup, restructuring, and bulk data migration are itemized additions. A beautiful one-page concept never turns a larger existing business into a Card quote.

THE QUOTEFootprint tier + capabilities + transition work + outside provider costs. A visual teaser may show direction; only a coverage-complete scope produces a customer price.

CRAFT & SCOPEEvery base tier is hand-built and responsive. The tier sets the delivered public footprint; original art, motion, tools, providers, collections, and transition work are selected separately so unused capabilities are never buried in a larger bundle.

CONTENT CAPACITYThe base footprint includes layout and light editing of client-supplied, approved facts and content up to the stated capacity. Additional content shaping covers capped light drafting/editing from those supplied facts; interviews, research, regulated claims, substantial net-new writing, and bulk entry are custom scope.

FORM CAPACITYSite through Flagship have capacity for at most one basic form only when it is explicitly named in the written scope; no form is inferred solely from page count. Card and Card Plus may add one bounded basic form.

HOW PAGES COUNTEach public page or post URL counts toward the tier’s page allowance. Collection entries can instead appear together as cards on an included collection page.

DENSITY STILL COUNTSPage capacity is a ceiling, not the only sizing axis. A higher band may serve fewer pages when its section, layout, content, media, form, or revision capacity is the honest fit.

OWNERSHIP & HIVEAfter final payment, Site Sourcery’s original custom deliverables for the approved scope transfer to you. Client-supplied and third-party material keeps its existing ownership or license. Account control and outside costs are recorded separately. The proposed managed Hive remains unavailable.
