The Sacco family story

More than a sub shop. A Shore ritual.

The Sacco family's sandwich tradition began in Atlantic City in 1947. Twenty-two years later, Sack O' Subs opened in Ventnor—bringing the same generously stacked submarine sandwiches to the Downbeach community.

The kind of place where your order becomes “the usual” and every summer tastes like the first one.
