Start to finish

Five steps. You do the first one.

01

Tell us about it

Tell us your business in a few taps — name, trade, town, and the one thing you want people to do. About thirty seconds. Rather talk it through, or not a fan of forms? A real person is one tap away, on every screen.

02

We build it

We build your site from scratch — your words, your photos, laid out for your trade — and built so Google and AI can find you and hand you over. Not a template with your logo dropped on top.

 same care as daarx.money · a real person, not a robot

03

A real person checks every word.

Before anything goes live under your name, a person makes sure it looks right and that every claim on it is true and yours. That's why nothing half-finished, and nothing made-up, ever goes out with your name on it. It's a feature, not a delay.

04

We put it online

We put it online, wire up tap-to-call and booking, and get you listed so people — and AI — actually find you. It's live at yourname.sitesourcery.com right away; your own web address follows whenever you want.

05

We keep it updated

New hours? New photos? A price change? Just text us — small changes like that are free, forever. You never log into anything. And once a month, one plain note lands: how the site did.
