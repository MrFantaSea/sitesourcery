2 Never missing a call · The Responder

You miss the call. It catches it.

The Responder is the first app of the Hive, our AI integration & automation menu. The moment a call goes unanswered, your phone texts the customer back — so they wait for you instead of dialing the next name in their search.

Beat 1 · The call

You can’t pick up

You’re on a ladder, under a sink, mid-cut. The call rings out and goes to voicemail — the moment most jobs quietly slip to the next name in the search.

Beat 2 · The text-back

Your phone texts them back

Within seconds, the caller gets a warm message in your voice — “Sorry I missed you, I’ll call you right back.” They know a real person saw them, so they hang on.

Beat 3 · The job

The job waits for you

You finish what you’re doing and call back on your own time. The customer’s still there — and the work is yours, not the competitor’s.
