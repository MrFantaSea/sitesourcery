What happens next

  1. You send this. It lands with the studio and is usually read the same business day; after-hours and weekend replies may take longer.
  2. We reply and scope it. We clarify the fit, tier, deliverables, and timing before sending a plain proposal.
  3. Commercial review comes first. An inquiry cannot start work. A project may begin only after a coverage-complete scope, written authorization, and any required payment.
  4. We build; you inspect. The custom site is made by hand, reviewed by you, and never goes live under your name until you approve it.
  5. Ownership is written precisely. Original custom deliverables for an approved scope transfer after final payment; supplied and third-party material keeps its existing ownership or license.
